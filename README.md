# Panam

> **"Panam" (பணம்) means "money" in Tamil.**
> Your self-hostable, all-in-one companion for personal finance and seamless group expense splitting.

Managing your money and splitting bills shouldn't be a hassle. **Panam** simplifies how you track your personal spending habits and settle up with friends—all in one beautiful, simple, and intuitive application designed to be completely self-hosted using Firebase.

---

## ✨ What Panam Can Do For You

Whether you're tracking your monthly grocery budget, splitting rent with roommates, or organizing a weekend trip with friends, Panam keeps everything transparent and organized. 

### 📊 Personal Finance Dashboard
- **Smart Tracking**: Quickly log your daily income and expenses. 
- **Visual Insights**: Monitor your spending habits with dynamic weekly and monthly pie and bar charts.
- **Budget Overview**: Stay on top of your financial goals at a glance from your personalized home dashboard.
- **Custom Categories**: Group your transactions seamlessly to suit your exact lifestyle and spending habits.

### 🤝 Effortless Group Expense Splitting
- **Dynamic Splitting Options**: Split bills exactly how you want to. Divide expenses evenly, by exact percentage, by custom amounts, or even by shares.
- **Group Ledgers**: Keep track of who owes who across all your different friend groups, roommates, and travel buddies. No more mental math or spreadsheet chaos.
- **Clear Balances**: See instantly exactly who you owe and who owes you in green and red bold text.
- **Settle Up**: Clear your debts in a tap and keep all your ledgers balanced.

### 🔔 Keep Everyone in the Loop
- **Instant Notifications**: Receive immediate alerts in your notification center whenever an expense is added or when someone settles up with you.
- **Cloud Synced**: Say goodbye to manual syncing. Your transaction data is safely backed up and synced in real-time with Firebase.

---

## 📱 How It Works

It's as easy as paying for a cup of coffee:

1. **Getting Started**: Create your secure account. Your data is synced automatically.
2. **Log Personal Spending**: Head over to the **Money** tab to log that solo morning coffee or grocery run. 
3. **Form a Group**: Going out to dinner? Create a group, share the Group ID with your friends so they can join, and easily log the receipt.
4. **Relax and Settle Up**: Let Panam do the complex math. When it's time to pay the Piper, check the group balances and settle your share smoothly.

## 🛠 For Developers

Want to peek underneath the hood or contribute to Panam? The app is built with modern web technologies and wrapped for native performance.

**Tech Stack:**
- **UI Framework:** [Ionic React](https://ionicframework.com/react)
- **Backend & Auth:** [Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/) (Firestore)
- **Visualization:** [Chart.js](https://www.chartjs.org/) & react-chartjs-2
- **Native Tooling:** Capacitor

### Getting Started Locally

1. Install module dependencies:
   ```bash
   npm install
   ```

2. Make sure you have your Firebase connection strings properly set up in your local `.env`.

3. Fire up the development server:
   ```bash
   npm run dev
   ```
